# StoryWeaver - The Multimodal Director 🎬

StoryWeaver is an AI creative director that generates rich, interleaved multimedia narratives. It combines text, images, and video into a seamless storytelling experience, all powered by Google's latest AI technologies.

## 🌟 Features

- **Multimodal Storytelling**: Real-time generation of narratives with embedded images and videos
- **Director's Console**: Fine-tune visual style, narrative tone, and pacing
- **Live Asset Generation**: Images and videos generated on-demand as the story unfolds
- **Beautiful UI**: Cinematic interface with smooth animations using Framer Motion
- **Session Management**: Save and share your created stories
